Recent Projects

Trump’s treatment in the Opinion Section of the NYTimes

Performed sentiment analysis on over 700 Op-Eds written between 2017 and 2018 and the corresponding 300,000 comments, finding that comments relating to Trump are significantly more negative than those not relating to him.

Wikipedia Talk pages: toxic and obscene?

PUsed machine learning classifiers to differentiate between normal, toxic and obscene comments to build 3rd best classifier in class.

Who gets the Academy Award?

Exploratory data analysis with actor nominations for Academy Awards with a focus on data collection (web-scraping, APIs) and visualisation, findings showing i.a. that women tend to be significantly younger than men at the peak of their career.

PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds: Is cheating contagious?

Created network simulations based on PlayerUnknown’s Battleground match data. Tested hypothesis that being killed by a cheater makes someone more likely to cheat, with findings rejecting the hypothesis.
